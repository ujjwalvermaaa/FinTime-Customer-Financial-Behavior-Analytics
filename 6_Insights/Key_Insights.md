# Key Insights  
## FinTime – Customer Financial Behavior Analytics  

1. Premium customers contribute the majority of revenue, creating dependency risk.  
2. Standard segment shows the highest churn vulnerability.  
3. Revenue exhibits strong seasonal patterns.  
4. Peak transaction activity occurs between 6 PM – 9 PM.  
5. Metro cities dominate revenue contribution.  
6. CLV varies significantly across segments.  
7. Churned customers represent substantial revenue at risk.
