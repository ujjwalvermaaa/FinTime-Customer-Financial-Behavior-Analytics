# Key Performance Indicator (KPI) Definitions  
## FinTime – Customer Financial Behavior Analytics  

---

| KPI Name | Definition | Business Purpose |
|--------|------------|------------------|
| Total Customers | Count of unique customers | Measure customer base |
| Total Transactions | Count of all transactions | Activity volume |
| Total Revenue | Sum of transaction amounts | Financial performance |
| Avg Transaction Value | Revenue ÷ Transactions | Spending efficiency |
| Active Customers | Customers active in last 90 days | Engagement health |
| Churn Rate (%) | Inactive customers ÷ Total | Retention risk |
| Revenue at Risk | Revenue from churned customers | Financial exposure |
| Customer Lifetime Value | Long-term revenue contribution | Prioritization |
| Financial Health Score | RFM-based composite score | Risk monitoring |
