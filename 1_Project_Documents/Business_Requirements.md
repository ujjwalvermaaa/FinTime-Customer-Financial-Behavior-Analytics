# Business Requirements & Project Scope  
## FinTime – Customer Financial Behavior Analytics  

**Author:** Ujjwal Verma  

---

## 1. Project Objectives

- Analyze customer transaction behavior at an individual level  
- Identify churn risk and revenue exposure  
- Segment customers based on value and engagement  
- Monitor financial health using behavioral metrics  
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard  
- Deliver actionable business insights and recommendations  

---

## 2. In-Scope Activities

- Transaction-level data analysis  
- Data cleaning and transformation using Python and SQL  
- Feature engineering and derived tables creation  
- Customer segmentation and churn classification  
- KPI design and validation  
- Power BI dashboard development  
- Insight documentation and business reporting  

---

## 3. Out-of-Scope Activities

- Real-time data pipelines  
- Machine learning deployment  
- Fraud detection systems  
- External data integrations  

---

## 4. Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibility |
|------------|----------------|
| Leadership | Strategic decisions & performance tracking |
| Marketing Team | Retention & engagement strategies |
| Risk Team | Churn and revenue risk monitoring |
| Analytics Team | Data modeling & reporting |

---

## 5. Success Metrics

| Metric | Expected Outcome |
|------|----------------|
| Churn Reduction | 10–20% improvement |
| Revenue Growth | Increased high-value contribution |
| Risk Visibility | Clear revenue-at-risk insights |
| Decision Efficiency | Faster data-driven decisions |
