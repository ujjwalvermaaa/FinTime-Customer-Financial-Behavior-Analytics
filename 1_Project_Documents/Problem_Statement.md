## 1. Background

Financial institutions often lack granular visibility into customer transaction behavior, relying instead on aggregated metrics that delay risk identification.

---

## 2. Business Problem

- Late detection of churn signals  
- High dependency on premium customers  
- Ineffective customer segmentation  
- Revenue volatility due to reactive strategies  

---

## 3. Proposed Solution

FinTime provides a behavioral analytics system that transforms transaction data into insights related to:

- Customer engagement  
- Churn risk  
- Revenue contribution  
- Financial health  

All insights are delivered via an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## 4. Expected Outcomes

- Reduced churn through early intervention  
- Improved revenue stability  
- Better customer targeting  
- Data-driven business decisions
